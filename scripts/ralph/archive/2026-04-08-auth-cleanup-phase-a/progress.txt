## Codebase Patterns

<!-- Patterns gerais do projeto. Stories-especificas entram abaixo. -->

- **Auth modules path:** `src/server/core/auth/` (NAO `src/server/features/auth/` que e legado)
- **Auth components:** `src/client/components/auth/` — login-otp-form.tsx, signup-otp-form.tsx, verify-email-form.tsx, two-factor-challenge.tsx, onboarding-form.tsx
- **Cookie name:** auth usa `accessToken` (nao `access_token`)
- **Client components:** NUNCA importar `@/lib/auth` (barrel) em `use client` — importar direto de `@/lib/auth/auth-provider`
- **Auth passwordless only:** endpoints `register`, `login`, `forgotPassword`, `resetPassword`, `changePassword` foram REMOVIDOS do BACKEND. Mas as PAGINAS frontend `/register`, `/forgot-password`, `/reset-password/[token]`, `/login/verify-magic`, `/signup/verify-magic` ainda existem e sao candidatas a delete em Release 2.
- **Padrao Igniter.js:** controller → actions (query/mutation) → handler com authProcedure({ required: true })
- **Schema Prisma:** `prisma/schema.prisma`. Models: Organization (type pf/pj), User (emailVerified nullable), VerificationCode (OTPs nao hasheados, type=MAGIC_LINK)
- **dev server:** http://localhost:3000 — `curl -s /api/health`
- **Git mmap workaround:** repo em OneDrive as vezes corrompe pack — `git hash-object -w` + `git update-index --add --cacheinfo` como fallback

## Known issues da Release 1 (pre-existentes, nao blockers)

- **US-106B seed visibility:** controller usa prisma GLOBAL, rows de withTransaction invisiveis ao handler. Solucao futura: withCommittedSeed ou truncate strategy.
- **US-108 bootstrap contract mode:** nao existem response Zod schemas. Contract tests usam shape snapshots locais.
- **/signup prod flakiness 20%:** 2/10 curl timeouts em 21s — suspeita rate limit / Turnstile.

## Decisoes desta release (Release 2 Phase A)

- **Escopo reduzido:** apenas Phase A (US-201, 202, 203). Phases B/C/D BLOQUEADAS por muro de 14 dias de observacao.
- **Branch:** `ralph/auth-cleanup-phase-a` (rodando em `ralph/auth-platform-hardening` por ora).
- **Privacidade:** IP hashed (SHA256 com salt de IGNITER_APP_SECRET) antes de logar. Nunca logar email ou IP plaintext.
- **5 rotas candidatas confirmadas existentes:**
  1. `/register` — src/app/(auth)/register/page.tsx
  2. `/forgot-password` — src/app/(auth)/forgot-password/page.tsx
  3. `/reset-password/[token]` — src/app/(auth)/reset-password/[token]/
  4. `/login/verify-magic` — src/app/(auth)/login/verify-magic/page.tsx + LoginVerifyMagicClient.tsx
  5. `/signup/verify-magic` — src/app/(auth)/signup/verify-magic/page.tsx + SignupVerifyMagicClient.tsx

## Regras duras

- **FR-1 Release 1:** testes escritos contra codigo ATUAL, sem refactor.
- **FR-1 Release 2:** nenhuma rota pode ser deletada sem 14 dias de observacao previa.
- **Privacidade:** ipHashed sempre. Sem PII em logs.
